Who We Are

Micro Weld And Cutting Tools is a leading manufacturer and distributor of premium industrial tools and welding equipment, serving clients across India and international markets since 2018. Founded by Mr. Manish Goyal, we've grown from a small workshop in New Delhi to a nationally recognized brand in the industrial tools sector.

Our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction has been the cornerstone of our success. We specialize in cutting wheels, welding electrodes, power tools, and other essential industrial equipment that professionals trust for their demanding applications.
