Product Description

A cutting wheel is a reduced circular disk composed of bonded abrasive grains, sometimes referred to as a cut-off wheel. It is intended to cut through hard materials with little damage to the surface and is placed on power instruments such stationary cutting machines or angle grinders. High-quality abrasives are used in the production of Micro Weld And Cutting Tools cutting wheels, which are strengthened by fiberglass mesh.

They comply with international safety requirements while guaranteeing smoother operations, higher cutting rates, and longer tool life.
