About Us

Micro Weld And Cutting Tools

In the field of industrial equipment and consumables, Micro Weld And Cutting Tools is a reputable brand renowned for performance, quality, and dependability. We specialize in providing a wide range of top-notch products, including cutting wheels, welding electrodes, power tools, marble blades, TCT saw blades, cleaning tools, and cordless tools.

Founded to meet the growing needs of the manufacturing, fabrication, and construction sectors, we have built a solid reputation through our integrity, technical expertise, and customer-first approach. Whether you are a small workshop or a major industrial client, you can expect the same level of commitment and service excellence from us.
